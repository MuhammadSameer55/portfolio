Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our SEO pricing and services

What are monthly SEO charges?

Our SEO charges vary based on your target market and requirements. Pakistan plans start from Rs 30,000/month, Gulf plans from Rs 50,000/month, and Tier-1 plans from Rs 70,000/month. All plans include comprehensive SEO services with guaranteed results.

How much time do you take to rank a website on Google?

Typically, we start seeing improvements within 2-3 months, with significant rankings achieved in 4-6 months. However, this varies based on competition, keyword difficulty, and your website's current state. We provide monthly progress reports to track your rankings.

Do you have any SEO clients outside Pakistan?

Yes, we have successful SEO campaigns running in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and other countries. Our team has extensive experience in international SEO and understands the nuances of different markets.

What kind of ROI can I expect from SEO?

SEO typically delivers 3-5x ROI within the first year, with increasing returns over time. Unlike paid advertising, SEO provides long-term sustainable results. The exact ROI depends on your industry, competition, and the value of your products/services.

How do you handle Google algorithm updates?

We use only white-hat SEO techniques that comply with Google's guidelines. Our strategies are future-proof and designed to withstand algorithm updates. We continuously monitor changes and adjust our strategies to ensure your rankings remain stable.
